What we do

Our digital classrooms are a proposal that integrates satellite internet and state-of- the-art educational technology, in order to encourage teachers and students towards educational innovation. Our platform includes training and active support for teachers and students with digital educational content, which are adapted to the needs of each school or community. These educational tools have the processes of implementation, monitoring, and metrics of qualitative and quantitative impact, in order to transform conventional classrooms, into classrooms that prepare teachers and students to develop the necessary skills to meet the challenges of the 21st century. With our digital classrooms we seek to reach the most remote corners of the country providing opportunities for development and equality in the places that most need quality education.
