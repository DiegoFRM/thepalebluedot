Board Members

Monica Aguirre

Monica Aguirre is currently CEO of Stargroup, a telecommunications and satellite services company in México. Prior to her current position she served as CFO of the same group, and also has experience in Investment Banking at BBVA Bancomer. Monica is Mexican and has an MBA from the Instituto de Empresa Business School in Madrid.

Cecilia Dosal

Cecilia Dosal is currently a Commercial Developer for New Fortress Energy in Mexico, an investment fund dedicated to the development of infrastructure in the energy sector. Previously, she collaborated in Enel both in the Commercial Office area and in Regulatory Affairs. In addition, she was a public policy consultant at Fundación IDEA / C230 and worked on fundraising and community development strategies with TECHO in the offices of Mexico and Chile. Cecilia is an economist from the Universidad Iberoamericana and has a Master´s Degree in Public Administration from Columbia University.

Cecilia Turriago

Cecilia Turriago is a Founding Partner and Executive Director of Enercar, an energy finance consultancy based in Mexico City. Previously, she served as Managing Director of Capital Investment in Latin America at GE Capital Energy Financial Services. Cecilia is Mexican / Colombian and has a Master´s degree in international studies from the University of Warwick in the United Kingdom and a Master´s degree in international economics from Johns Hopkins University (SAIS).
